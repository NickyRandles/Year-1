Episode 8 - Gurgle

Date Aired: 4th of June 2009

My personal rating: 7.5/10

Recap

Sheriff Mills questions JD in prison about Cole Harkin and JD tells him his whereabouts in the woods. Sheriff Mills goes to the woods but hits a trip wire and is hit by an arrow in the thigh. When he comes through he's in Cole's cabin and Cole tells him that he believes there is someone imitating Wakefield's crimes as he found a journal which would make it easy to do so. Before he can say anymore Cole is killed. Elsewhere, J.D. escapes from prison after Deputy Garrett is shot dead. Meanwhile, most of the wedding guests make it off the island but the main guests stay behind to look for the missing Madison. Whilst out looking for Madison, Henry & Abby find Richard's speared body masted to a tree and straight after Abby receives a call from Madison telling her if the rest of the guests leave the island Madison will be killed. Jimmy & Abby rescue Sheriff Mills and goes down to the marina where Abby finds a stabbed J.D. who tells her the killings are all about her before he dies. From behind her a distraught Henry appears covered in J.D.'s blood.
