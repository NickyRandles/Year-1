Harper's Island

Welcome to the Harper's Island website!

Harper's Island was a tv show that debuted on April 9th 2009. The show only had one series which ended on July 11th 2009. The show had 13th episodes which were full of action, horror and drama! In each episode some of the characters are killed by a mysterious killer. The show is fully of mystery, and all is revealed in the dramatic end.

About Harper's Island plot

Harpers Island is a horror tv series about a group of family and friends that go to a remote island to have a wedding. They have come to have a good time but little do they know that the majority of them are going to end up as murder victims. When they arrive there is a happy wedding atmosphere soon but dies down as one by one, members of the group are killed. The wedding week of fun turns into fight for survival for the remaining members of the group. This isn't the first time that a murder spree has taken place on the island. One of the main, Abby Mill's mother was killed in a murder spree seven years ago. Abby is returning to the island for the first time since the incident for her friend Henry's wedding. Henry Dunn is planning on marrying the girl of his dreams, Trish Wellington, Who is a beautiful and wealthy girl. The wedding week turns bad when people start to die. In every episode, atleast one person dies. The victims are mainly wedding guests and island locals. The killer is very smart and leaves few traces behind. Everyone is a suspect and no one trusts anyone. No one is safe and anyone could be next. At the end of the series, all questions will be answered and the killer will be revealed. Only the strong will survive and there will be few survivors!
