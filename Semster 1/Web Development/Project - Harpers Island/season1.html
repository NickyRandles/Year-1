Harpers Island - Season 1

The was only one season of Harper's Island which consisted of 13 episodes. The episodes where all 45 minutes long. The episodes originally ran from the 9th of April to the 11th of July.

Season 1 overview

Season 1 of Harper's island was a series full of mystery, drama and horror. The series starts of with a group of family and friends who are going off to a remote island to celebrate the wedding of Trish Wellington and Henry Dunn. The atmosphere at the start of the series is a very happy atmosphere and it is clear that the group is unaware of the horrible things they are going to experience when they first take off. The atmosphere dramatically changes as the series goes on. Deaths occur right from the get go. Each episode consists of alot of drama and alot of deaths! As the episode go on, the tenison increases and the holiday become a fight for survival. Many will die and only the strong will survive! Nobody trusts anybody, and everyone is a suspect. In the end, all questions will be answered and the killer will be revealed!
