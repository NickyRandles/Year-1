Christopher Gorham (Henry Dunn)

Christopher Gorham is an actor who plays the character Henry Dunn in Harper's Island. He was born on the 14th of August 1974 in Fresno, California, USA.

About his character Henry Dunn

Henry Dunn is going to the island to marry the love of his life Trish Wellington. He is best friends with Abby Mills. Both of his parents died years ago and his only living relatives are his unlce Marty and brother J.D.
