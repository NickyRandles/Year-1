Brandon Jay McLaren (Danny Brooks)

Brandon Jay McLaren is an actor who plays thee role of Danny Brooks in harper's island. He was born on the 3rd of July 1981 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.

About his character Danny Brooks

Danny is one of Henry's groomsmen. Henry and him have been best friends since college. Danny is a nice guy and he is very sensitive. He tries to make everything right and he likes to help people out. He is very good friends with Sully and he is also quite close with Booth and Malcolm .His kindness helps him attract many friend on the island.
