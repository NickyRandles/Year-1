Cameron Richardson (Chloe Carter)

Cameron Richardson is an actress who plays the character Chloe Carter in harpers island. She was born on the 11th of September 1979 in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, USA.

About her character Chloe Carter

Chloe is one of Trish's bridesmaids. She is going out with Cal. She is an attractive girl an she attractive the attention of Marty and Sully. She show that she is truly in love with Cal by turning them both down. She seems to be very interested in the John Wakefield case and she asks some of her friends to go visit his grave.
