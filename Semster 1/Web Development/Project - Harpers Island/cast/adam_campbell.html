Adam Campbell (Cal Vandeusen)

Adam Campbell is an actor who plays the role of Cal Vandeusen in harper's island. He was born the 7th of November 1980 in Bath, Somerset, England, UK.

About his character Cal Vandeusen

Cal Vandeusen is from England and he is a doctor. He was invited to the wedding by his girlfriend Chloe. He does know any of the others going on the trip and he is abit of an outsider. He tries to fit in more by befriending Henry and some of his friends.He is deeply in love with Chloe and he by her an engagement ring with the intention of proposing to her on the trip.
