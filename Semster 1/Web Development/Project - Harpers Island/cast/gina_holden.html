Gina Holden (Shea Allen)

Gina Holden is an actress that plays character of Shea Allen in harpers island. She was born on the 17th of March 1975 in Smithers, British Columbia, Canada.

About her character Shea Allen

Shea Allen is Trish's big sister and she is the maid of honour. She is married and she has a daughter called Madison. She seems to have the perfect life but she has alot of personal problems with her family.
