Katie Cassidy (Trish Wellington)

Katie Cassidy is an actress who plays the character Tish Wellington in harper's island. Trish was born on 26th of November 1986 in Los Angeles, California, USA.

About her character Trish Wellington

Trish is going to the island to marry her finace Henry Dunn. Trish comes from a wealthy family and her father Thomas Wellington doesn't was her to marry Henry. He tries to sabotage the wedding by trying to reunite Trish with her old boyfriend Hunter.
