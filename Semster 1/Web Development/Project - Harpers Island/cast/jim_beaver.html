Jim Beaver (Sheriff Charlie Mills)

Jim Beaver is an actor who plays the character Sheriff Charlie Mills. He was born on the 12th of August in Laramie, Wyoming, USA

About his character Sheriff Charlie Mills

Sheriff Mills is the local sheriff on the island. He is Abby Mills father. He claims he killed John Wakefield seven years ago after he killed his wife and many others. He then send his daughter off the island and this is her first time back in seven year for he friend Henry Dunns wedding.
