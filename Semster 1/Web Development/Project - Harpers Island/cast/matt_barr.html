Matt Barr (Christopher 'Sully' Sullivan )

Matt Barr is an actor who plays the character Christopher 'Sully' Sullivan. Matt was born on the 14th of February 1984 in Texas, USA.

About his character Christopher 'Sully' Sullivan

Christopher is going to be Henry's best man for his wedding. Christopher and Henry have been best friends since middle school.Christopher is called Sully by his friends.He is the fun one of the group and he loves to party. Some of his friends think he is more excited for the bachelor party than the wedding.
