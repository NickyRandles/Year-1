Cassandra Sawtell (Madison Allen)

Cassandra Sawtell is an actress who play the role of Madison Allen in harper's island. She was born in 1997 in Canada.

About her character Madison Allen

Madison is the daughter of Shea and Richard Allen. She is going to be the flower girl for Trish and Henry's wedding. She seems to be a quite disturbed girl as she seems to her and see spooky things. She is kidnapped and when is found she tells everyone that Sheriff Mills kidnapped her. She then confesses to Trish that it was John Wakefield who kidnapped her. He told that her mother would be killed if she didn't keep it a secret.
