Elaine Cassidy (Abby Mills)

Elaine Cassidy is an actress who plays the character Abby Mills in harper's island. Elaine was born Kilcoole, County Wicklow on 31st of December 1979.

About her character Abby Mills

Abby Mills is the main character of Harper's Island. Abby is returning back to the island for the first time in seven years, for her best friend Henry Dunn's wedding. Seven years ago, Abby's mother was killed along with many others by a serial killer named John Wakefield.
